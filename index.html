About Zaide

Founded in 2020 by a team of AI experts and customer service veterans, Zaide emerged from a simple observation: businesses needed a better way to manage customer communications across multiple channels. Our founders combined their expertise in machine learning and customer experience to create a platform that would revolutionize how businesses interact with their customers.

Our mission is to democratize access to advanced AI-powered customer service tools, making enterprise-level technology accessible to businesses of all sizes. We believe in the power of human-AI collaboration and are committed to developing solutions that enhance rather than replace human interaction. Our core values of innovation, accessibility, and customer-centricity drive everything we do.
